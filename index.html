Welcome to Janey's Pie Shop

Our history

For many years, Janey has been baking the most delicious pies at her home.

In 2013, we opened our first store in Dublin in a cosy little street near St. Stephen's Green. People from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Janey's Pie Shop moved in to a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this our very own online store, making it possible to order Janey's delicious pies whenever you feel like it.
